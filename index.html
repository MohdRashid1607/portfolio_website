// How I Work

My Design Process

A structured, iterative approach — from first idea to final launch.

01

Discover

Deep research into the brand, target audience, and market. I define goals, constraints, and success metrics before writing a single line of code.

02

Design

From wireframes to high-fidelity mockups. Iterating on layouts, typography and colour systems until the design elegantly solves the problem.

03

Build

Clean, semantic code with performance and accessibility in mind. Every component tested across devices and browsers before moving on.

04

Launch

Deployment, SEO setup, and analytics integration. Post-launch review included — because great work always evolves.
